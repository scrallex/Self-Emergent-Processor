The Physics Behind SEP

Grounded in real mathematics and quantum information theory

Prime-Indexed Recursion

Time advances only at prime-numbered steps (2, 3, 5, 7, 11...), creating irreducible computational beats that inject novelty and prevent trivial periodicity.

Quantum Coherence

Information states maintain phase relationships through Q-value coherence metrics, enabling quantum-like superposition and interference in classical computation.

Combinatorial Growth

Each additional unit exponentially expands the state space while energy requirements scale logarithmically, following Landauer's principle of information thermodynamics.

Emergent Complexity

Simple recursive rules generate complex, self-organizing patterns that mirror natural systems from quantum mechanics to biological intelligence.
